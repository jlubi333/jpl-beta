About Me

I am Justin, a high schooler with a passion for computer science and math. I have been programming for four years, and plan to do so for many more. In college, my plans are to major in computer science and mathematics and eventually obtain a PhD in computer science with the intention of becoming a computer science professor.

I strive for minimalist beauty in all my works. My love for minimalism extends beyond clean design; an outstanding and intuitive user experience as a whole is of utmost importance to me. I will not, however, hesitate to implement complex solutions for complex problems because—let’s face it—reality is never simple.

In my free time, I like to play the piano and fence.
